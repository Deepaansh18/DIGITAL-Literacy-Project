# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## Safety Tips
- We should use strong passwords which should contain letters, numbers, and symbols.
- Do not share OTPs, passwords and personal details with anyone.
- Always verify links before clicking.
- Enable two-factor authentication on your accounts.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for important transactions.
- Always keep your phone and apps updated with the latest security updates.

## UPI / Financial Safety
- Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
- Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money.
